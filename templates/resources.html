Crisis Resources
Important: If you're experiencing a mental health emergency or having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out for immediate help.
988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free and confidential support for people in distress, 24/7.

Call or Text: 988

Online: 988lifeline.org

Crisis Text Line

Free, 24/7 text support with a trained Crisis Counselor.

Text: HOME to 741741

Online: crisistextline.org

Emergency Services

For immediate danger to yourself or others, contact emergency services.

Call: 911 (US)

Or go to your nearest emergency room

Finding Professional Help

While self-help resources are valuable, sometimes professional support is needed. Here are some ways to find mental health professionals:

Therapist Directories
  • Psychology Today Therapist Directory
  • American Association of Marriage and Family Therapists
  • Anxiety and Depression Association of America
Telehealth Options
  • Talkspace - Online therapy
  • BetterHelp - Online counseling
  • Amwell - Telehealth including therapy
Community Resources
  • Community mental health centers
  • University psychology departments
  • Hospital departments of psychiatry
  • Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)
Low-Cost Options
  • Open Path Psychotherapy Collective - Affordable therapy
  • Sliding scale fee therapists
  • Training clinics at universities
  • Community health centers
